About Us

M. A. Idaiye & Co. is a reputable and client-focused law firm located at 96 First East Circular Road, Sakponba Junction, Benin City, Edo State. With a commitment to excellence, integrity, and personalized legal services, our firm stands out as a trusted partner in navigating the complexities of the legal system in Nigeria.

We provide a broad range of legal services tailored to meet the diverse needs of individuals, families, and businesses. Whether you're drafting a will, handling property disputes, pursuing justice in court, or seeking legal guidance on contracts and agreements, M. A. Idaiye & Co. is here to help you every step of the way.

At M. A. Idaiye & Co., our practice is rooted in professionalism, respect for clients, and a thorough understanding of Nigerian law. We leverage our legal expertise and real-world experience to offer practical solutions that protect your rights and secure your interests.
